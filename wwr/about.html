About Noble Rafting

Providing safe and unforgettable river experiences since our founding. We combine expert guides, modern equipment, and friendly service.

Our Story

Noble Rafting was started by a small group of river-lovers who wanted to share the joy of the river with others. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted local outfitter focused on safety and guest experience.

We continually train our staff and invest in modern safety gear so guests can enjoy the adventure with confidence.

Trip Summaries

Half Day Adventure

2–3 hours on mild to moderate rapids, great for families and beginners. Includes safety briefing and basic paddling instruction.

Full Day Expedition

Full-day route with lunch stop, ideal for nature lovers wanting a longer river experience.

Sunset Raft

Evening float with calm stretches and scenic sunset views. Perfect for couples and photography.
