Equipment & Safety

Included for All Trips

  • USCG-style life jacket or equivalent
  • Helmet (for rapids sections)
  • Paddle and safety briefing
  • Experienced certified guide

Recommended to Bring

  • Quick-dry clothes and water shoes
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses with strap
  • Small personal water and snacks (unless full-day provided)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age?
Minimum age for most runs is 8; family runs may allow younger children with prior approval.
Are reservations required?
Reservations are recommended — small groups fill quickly on weekends and holidays.
Do I need prior rafting experience?
No experience is necessary for easy and family trips. Full-day or rapid runs require moderate experience.
What should I bring?
Wear quick-dry clothes, water shoes, sunscreen, and bring a water bottle. Life jackets and helmets are provided.
